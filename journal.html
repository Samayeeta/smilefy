Journal Entry

Finding a healthy manner to express yourself is one of the strategies to deal with any overwhelming feeling. As a result, keeping a journal can help you manage your mental health. Journaling can assist you in the following ways:

Creating a space for positive self-talk and recognising negative ideas and behaviours.

Keeping track of any symptoms day-to-day so you can learn to detect triggers and better control them.
