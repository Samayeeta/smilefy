Suicide Helpline Numbers

It's vital to have a conversation about mental health. However, it can be challenging for certain people and might trigger powerful emotional responses. Smilefy advises you to seek help from your local health care facility, a local mental health community organisation, or a national helpline (if one is available). Giving it a go has nothing to lose. Calling a suicide hotline is a great method to express yourself while also getting helpful suggestions on how to handle your problems. If you want to help yourself or someone else, this is a great place to start. The list below includes a thorough list of nations as well as the phone numbers for their respective suicide hotlines.
