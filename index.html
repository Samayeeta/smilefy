Welcome to Project Smilefy

Smilefy's aim is to empower individuals dealing with mental health issues by providing support, education, and assistance. We accomplish this by providing a safe environment in which those coping with mental health illnesses can obtain support. Mental health concerns, we believe, have an impact on societal challenges such as education, homelessness, and poverty.
